# INTRODUCTION

## Why This Guide Exists

Buying a car should feel exciting. Instead, it often feels overwhelming, opaque, and stressful.

This guide exists to bring structure, clarity, and independence to that process — so you can make the right decision with confidence.

This is not about “winning” at negotiation.  
It’s about thinking clearly before you ever step into a dealership.
